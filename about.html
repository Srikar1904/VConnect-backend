About V-Connect

V-Connect is a digital governance platform designed to empower citizens by enabling them to report local problems using real-time images. The system ensures transparency, accountability, and efficient resolution of village-level issues.

Objectives

  • Enable image-based problem reporting
  • Improve transparency in fund utilization
  • Reduce delays in grievance resolution
  • Strengthen citizen–government trust

Who Benefits?

Citizens, Sarpanch, Village Officers, and Government Authorities benefit from a unified digital platform.
