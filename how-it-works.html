How It Works

Step-by-step guide to report problems

Workflow of V-Connect

  1. Citizen Login – Citizen logs in using registered mobile number.
  2. Problem Reporting – Uploads image, location, and description.
  3. Sarpanch Review – Verifies and initiates resolution.
  4. Work Completion – After-image uploaded as proof.
  5. Admin Approval – Status updated with fund details.
  6. Citizen Notification – Citizen receives updates.

This ensures transparency, accountability, and faster resolution.
