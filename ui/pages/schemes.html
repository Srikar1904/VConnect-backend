Relevant Government Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)

PMGSY aims to provide all-weather road connectivity to unconnected rural areas. Road damage complaints in V-Connect may be resolved using funds allocated under this scheme.

Ministry: Ministry of Rural Development

Official Website

Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin)

This scheme focuses on sanitation, drainage systems, and waste management in rural areas. Garbage and drainage complaints are linked to this initiative.

Ministry: Ministry of Jal Shakti

Official Website

Jal Jeevan Mission

Ensures safe and adequate drinking water supply through household tap connections. Water supply complaints are addressed under this scheme.

Ministry: Ministry of Jal Shakti

Official Website

MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act)

Provides employment for rural infrastructure development including drainage, roads, and public works. Many village-level works are funded through MGNREGA.

Ministry: Ministry of Rural Development

Official Website

Gram Panchayat Development Plan (GPDP)

Annual planning and budgeting framework for Gram Panchayats. Street lights, minor repairs, and village infrastructure are funded through GPDP allocations.

Authority: State Panchayat Raj Department
