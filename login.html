Why V-Connect?

V-Connect allows citizens to report real-time problems in their streets by uploading images. These issues are reviewed by village authorities and resolved transparently with proper fund tracking.

Our platform bridges the gap between citizens and local government, ensuring every grievance is heard and addressed efficiently.
